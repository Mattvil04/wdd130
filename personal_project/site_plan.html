Content

Home page

[I-work is the website of work that plan to help people that are unemployed to find a job from your home based on your talents and skills, this page offer a secure space where the clients publish proyects and where the people can apply. In this way the client can hire the person that the company needs according to the talents, skills or services that they need or they are looking for. Our mission is "Accomplish a better efficiency by getting jobs quickly from anywhere in the world" This means that its aim is to improve the employment conditions and work skills of workers affected by employment problems, to promote their integration or reintegration into the formal, salaried or independent labor market and to improve their employment conditions. The I-work website will try to connect employees who are looking for work or are interested in improving their skills for work. this website will allow: online access will allow online and direct access through any electronic device with internet service; will allow registration, navigation and access to services and functions independently; and will be In constant development and updating: its functions and services will be reviewed and improved according to the needs and possibilities that arise during its use.]
